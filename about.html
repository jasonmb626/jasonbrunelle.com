About Me

Check Out My Portfolio

I discovered my love of computers at a young age. I've been programming since the age of 11 and setting up Linux and Windows machines since the age of 19. 20+ years later I have as much passion as I ever had. With 15 years management experience on top of this, I also bring maturity, dedication, and professionalism to the team.

I recently graduated--with a degree in Computer Science--from the University of Houston Downtown, relocating to Chicago, Illinois shortly thereafter. Full stack web development combines my passions for programming, system administration, and making applications that can benefit anyone on their desktop or mobile.

My main language is JavaScript. It's been interesting watching as it has grown from a language we only tolerated because we didn't have anything better into a language we seek out even for back-end development, data scraping, and console applications.
